You begin to think, maybe erroneously,
that this other kind of anger is really a
type of knowledge: the type that both
clarifies and disappoints. It responds to
insult and attempted erasure simply by
asserting presence, and the energy required
to present, to react, to assert is accompanied
by visceral disappointment: a disappointment
in the sense that no amount of visibility
will alter the ways in which one is perceived.

Yes, and this is how you are a citizen.
